Fritatta

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • vegetables (broccoli, onion, bell pepper, potato, etc.)
  • cheese (anything that melts well)
  • cured meat (bacon, prosciutto, etc.)
  • salt, pepper, cayenne

Instructions

  1. Eggs and vegetables should have roughly equal volume.
  2. In a large skillet, fry vegetables until cooked then add cured meat.
  3. Whisk eggs in a bowl.
  4. Pour eggs over vegetables, stir together then top with cheese.
  5. Finish the dish under the broiler until the top is lightly browned.
